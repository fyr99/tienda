About Nike

Pushing the limits of innovation to make athletes better.

Our Mission

At Nike, our mission is to bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete in the world. If you have a body, you are an athlete. We aim to create products that help people reach their full potential, all while pushing the boundaries of sustainability and ethical business practices.

Our Core Values

Innovation

We are relentless in our pursuit of new and better ways to innovate in sports and beyond.

Inspiration

We believe in the power of sport to inspire, to break down barriers, and to unite people.

Sustainability

We are committed to using sustainable materials and reducing waste, to protect the planet for future generations.
